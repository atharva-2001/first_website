The Beauty of Mathematics

Suppose you were asked to talk to some extraterrestrial which somehow came to Earth. You might wonder,what language should I talk in?Well,you talk to the ET in the language of math and science, which are believed to be same throughout the cosmos.

Scientists have sent various objects in the space,to find some civilization out there,hoping we are not alone.For instance the Pioneer Plaques which were placed on board the Pioneer spacecraft,are written in the language of science(binary) and has information about us.

Mathematics is not just the study of how numbers work,but is an art and opens the wonders of the universe to the ones that love playing with it.

Some of my favorite things is mathematics is 4d objects and some non orientable objects(like klien bottles,mobius loops). My favorite set of numbers is the Fibonacci sequence,a beauty in itself.
